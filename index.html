South Africa

KwaZulu-Natal, a coastal South African province, is known for its beaches, mountains and savannah populated by big game. The safari destination Hluhluwe-iMfolozi Park is home to rhinos, lions and giraffes. Durban is an Indian-influenced harbor city and a popular surfing spot, and the Zulu culture is an important part of the region.
